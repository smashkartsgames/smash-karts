FAQ

Unblocked FAQ

Can I play Smash Karts unblocked at school?
In most cases, yes. Smash Karts runs entirely in the browser and doesn't require a download, so it isn't blocked by typical school content filters. If your school specifically blocks gaming domains, the video above walks through the workarounds that don't violate device policies.
Does Smash Karts work on Chromebook?
Yes. Chromebooks run Chrome, and Chrome supports WebGL — which is everything Smash Karts needs. No extension, no Linux mode, no developer toggles required. Open the tab and play.
Is Smash Karts Unblocked free?
100% free. There's no paid version of Smash Karts — "unblocked" just means hosted on a domain that isn't blocked at school. The game itself is identical to the main release.
Why is Smash Karts blocked at some schools?
Many schools use content filters that automatically block any domain categorized as "games" or "entertainment." This is usually a blanket policy, not a specific block on Smash Karts — and it changes based on which filter your district uses (GoGuardian, Lightspeed, Securly, etc.).
