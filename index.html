The Game

What is Smash Karts?

3-Minute Battles

Fast FFA or team matches. Whoever racks up the most kills before the clock hits zero takes the win.

11 Wild Weapons

Rockets, mines, machine guns, invincibility stars — grab a crate and unleash chaos every few seconds.

Fully Customizable

187 unlockable characters, hats and kart colors. Everything is earned in-game — nothing locked behind a paywall.

Smash Karts is a free-to-play multiplayer kart-battle game in the MOKBA (Massively Online Kart Battle Arena) genre. Built by indie studio Tall Team, it's playable instantly in any modern browser — no app store, no install, no signup. Matches are short, chaotic, and built around weapon pickups in tight arena maps.

The game is fully crossplay: PC and mobile players share the same lobbies, and new seasonal updates roll out every few weeks with fresh maps, weapons, and unlockables. Sessions are 3 minutes long, which makes it the perfect "one more game" loop between classes, on a break, or whenever you've got a few minutes to kill.

Getting Started

How to Play Smash Karts

1

Open in browser

No download. Hit play and the game loads instantly in any modern browser.

2

Drive with WASD

Or use the arrow keys. Hold shift to drift around tight corners and slingshot.

3

Grab weapon crates

Crates spawn around the arena. Roll over one to load up a random weapon.

4

Smash & win

Hit spacebar to fire. Most kills in 3 minutes takes the crown.

Pro Strategy

Tips & Tricks to Win Every Match

01

Keep Moving

Stopping is a death sentence. Even when reloading, keep drifting — a still kart is target practice.

02

Learn the Maps

Memorize where crates spawn. Camp those spots when you've got a fresh weapon ready to fire.

03

Save Your Shield

Don't pop the invincibility star the second you grab it. Save it for chokepoints and end-game rushes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Smash Karts?
Smash Karts is a free multiplayer kart-battle game playable directly in your browser. You drive a kart around a tight arena, grab weapons from crates, and try to rack up the most kills in a 3-minute match. Matches support up to 8 players.
Is Smash Karts free to play?
Yes — Smash Karts is 100% free. There's no paid version, no subscription, and no required signup. Cosmetic unlocks (karts, hats, characters) are all earned through gameplay.
Do I need to download Smash Karts?
No download required. The game runs entirely in your browser using WebGL. There are also iOS and Android apps if you prefer mobile, but the browser version has the full feature set with no install.
Can I play Smash Karts with friends?
Yes. Create a Private Room from the main menu, share the room link with your friends, and you'll be matched together. Up to 8 players can join a private lobby.
Is Smash Karts safe for kids?
The game is broadly kid-friendly — cartoon visuals, no graphic violence, and no real-money purchases. Voice chat is not built in. Match-text chat exists but is heavily filtered, and parents can disable chat in the settings.
