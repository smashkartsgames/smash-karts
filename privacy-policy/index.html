Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

SmashKarts Hub ("this site") is a static web site. We do not operate servers, run databases, or collect any personal information directly. This policy explains what third-party services are embedded on the site and what data they may collect.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no user accounts, contact forms, comment systems, or analytics scripts operated by us on this site.

3. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are embedded on this site and may collect data independently:

  • Adsterra — display advertising network. Adsterra may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve relevant ads. See Adsterra Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube (Google) — embedded video players. YouTube may collect data per Google's privacy policy when you interact with embedded videos. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may log font requests. See Google Fonts Privacy FAQ.
  • smashkarts.io — the game is embedded via iframe from smashkarts.io (Tall Team). Their privacy policy governs data collected during gameplay. See smashkarts.io.

4. Cookies

This site itself does not set cookies. Adsterra and YouTube embeds may set third-party cookies in your browser for advertising and analytics purposes. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We do not have user accounts or data submission forms of any kind.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made.
